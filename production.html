How Godzilla was Produced


Godzilla was Produced by Ishiro Honda, a japanese war veteran. The studio producing it would be Toho.
Originally, it was going to be a stop-motion picture, as per the vision of the head of effects ,Eiji Tsuburaya. his idea was it would be like the original King Kong movie, which made in stop-motion. This idea got vetoed due to the fact that it would take way to long to actually produce. Eventually, it was settled that they would use actors inside rubber suits to bring the character to life.

The Movie only took around 4 months to be filmed.
