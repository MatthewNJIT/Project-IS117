Godzilla's Box-Office Impact


Godzilla was given good reviews when it was first released on November 3,1954, and it was critically acclaimed when it was released in America during April 27, 1956.

Godzilla broke records on its opening day by selling the most tickets in Tokyo, and then sold a whooping 9.69 million ticket across its theatrical run! It earned a total of 2.8 million dollars during its theatrical run, with its budget being about $ 275,000. Quite the impressive run in all honestly.
