Cultural Importance of Godzilla


Godzilla is now recognized as one of the most influential movies to ever be created, and not just in Japan. The King of the Monsters has spawned what is known as the "Kaiju" film drama, and has endured for decades as the most iconic Kaiju.

His influence expands beyond Japan, as not only does he have a star on the hollywood Walk of Fame, he also now has entire american shared Universe of movies.

The Cinematic Universe, called the Monsterverse has been very successful despite only having 5 films from 2014 to 2024.
