Why Godzilla was Created


Godzilla serves as a grim reminder of the sheer destruction a single nuke can bring forth. Japan had been the target of two nuclear bombs that had been dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki, during the final days of World War 2. The effects of an entire cities destruction with a single bomb, and the death of thousands, was very profound.

Godzilla is Nuclear Destruction Incarnate, and is used to represent the Monster that was unleashed with the splitting of the Atom.
