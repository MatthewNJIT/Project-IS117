Plot of Godzilla


After the end of World War 2, Japan had been slowly rebuilding its destroyed foundations.
One day several fishing boats wash up mysteriously destroyed, but is written off as an accident by the Government. unknown to the people of Japan, the Nuclear Bomb testing in the Pacific had awoken a Horrific monster, Godzilla.

Furious for being awakened, and with a hunger for the destruction of humanity, the king of the Monsters emerges into the tokyo bay, intent on ravaging the massive city. Nothing could stand against Godzilla as he carved a path of destruction across Japan. All hope had seemed lost, as the Beast seemed invincible.

One brilliant scientist, Dr.Serizawa, created a super-weapon capable of killing Godzilla once and for all. dubbed the Oxygen Destroyer the bomb would detonate and destroy all the oxygen in the area, killing ALL life in the area.

Knowing such a weapon would unleash horrors on the world, Serizawa destroyed all the blueprints of the weapon and used it on godzilla with him detonating it in-person, killing both him and Godzilla.
